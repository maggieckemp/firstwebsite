About Me

Hello, I'm Maggie, and I'm passionate about harnessing the power of data to drive positive change in the environmental field. With a strong educational background in both mathematics and environmental studies, complemented by a year of graduate studies in data science, I'm committed to bridging the gap between data and sustainability.

More About Me

My journey has been a blend of academia and hands-on experience. As a graduate teaching assistant, I engaged with over 200 undergraduate students, demystifying complex ecological concepts and even teaching complete beginners how to use programming languages like R to create simulated ecosystems. My fascination with data led me to research the effects of extreme drought on prairies, where I analyzed large datasets, developed range maps, and contributed to a digital field guide for moths.

I believe that data holds the key to solving some of our most pressing environmental challenges. Like passionate individuals in the data science and environmental fields I admire, I'm dedicated to translating intricate data into actionable insights that support a greener future.

If you share this passion for leveraging data to address climate change and promote sustainability, let's connect. Together, we can make a meaningful impact.

Continuous Learning

In the ever-evolving world of technology and programming, I am committed to continuous learning and skill enhancement. Here are some of the resources and platforms I regularly engage with to stay up-to-date and further develop my programming expertise:

Lost At SQL

"Lost At SQL" proved to be a captivating and effective resource for brushing up on my SQL skills. This interactive coding story casts you as a ship captain, navigating a sinking vessel with no way out except through the ship's database records. Your mission: guide your crew to safety, unravel the mystery behind the sinking ship, identify potential culprits, and urgently search for an escape route. The engaging narrative and clear, step-by-step instructions make it an enjoyable and productive way to refresh and reinforce SQL proficiency. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced SQL user, "Lost At SQL" provides an immersive learning experience that combines storytelling with hands-on SQL practice.

DataLemur

Created by Nick Singh, the author of "Ace the Data Science Interview," DataLemur is a treasure trove of programming and statistics questions. It curates commonly asked technical interview questions from leading companies like Facebook, LinkedIn, Spotify, and more. DataLemur's structured approach equips me with the skills needed to ace technical round interviews. Immediate feedback on my code and insightful discussions on problem solutions make it an indispensable tool in my learning journey. By dedicating time each day to tackle challenges on DataLemur and LeetCode, I ensure that I stay updated with the latest programming trends and continually refine my problem-solving abilities—a crucial aspect of excelling in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
